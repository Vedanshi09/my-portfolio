Let's Talk Chat Gpt

The project conducted by my group focused on the implementation of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in education. Me and my group recognized the increasing use of AI technology in various aspects of life, including education, and aimed to explore its benefits and challenges. The research methodology involved conducting in-depth interviews with 17 participants, using a semi-structured approach to gather qualitative data. The interviews covered topics such as students' perspectives on AI in the classroom, experiences with AI tools, ethics of AI usage in education, and the long-term impact of AI on learning outcomes. The dataset collected provided diverse and valuable insights into the implementation of AI in education. The project aimed to gain a comprehensive understanding of the subject, considering multiple perspectives and experiences to shape the future of education.
